A Partnership That Brings Value to Your Community

The Long-Term Care Facility Partnership Program is a recurring monthly service designed specifically for nursing homes, assisted living communities, and memory care facilities. This is not a one-time event—it's a sustained program that brings professional portraiture, resident dignity, and family connection to your community each month.

How the Program Works

  • Professional, guided portrait sessions in a calm, respectful environment
  • Minimal disruption to facility operations and resident schedules

Environmental & Emotional Impact

Strengthening Family Connections

When families visit, they see their loved one beautifully portrayed in your facility—and they have the opportunity to create their own family keepsake. The photographer connects with family members when appropriate, offering them the option to purchase additional wall art, professional albums, or portraits for their own homes.

Important: Family purchases are completely optional and separate from your facility contract. Your monthly agreement includes only the framed portraits for facility display and resident/family copies.
